Do you have a student visa?

YES NO
Follow the steps below to transfer:
  1. Tell current school you want to transfer
  2. Get transfer form and application from new school
  3. Complete application and have transfer form signed by current school
  4. Have current school send transfer form to new school
  5. Submit application and payment to new school with documents:
    • passport copy
    • Copy of student visa in passport
    • Copy of current I-20
    • Copy of bank statement
  6. Attend orientation on start date at new school
 Follow the steps to apply and get an I-20:
  1. Complete school application
  2. Submit school application with following documents:
    • Photocopy of passport photo page
    • Copy of bank statement to show funding
    • Application fee
    • Payment for first session of course
  3. Receive I-20 from school
  4. Pay SEVIS fee
  5. Schedule student visa appointment
  6. Book flight after you receive student visa
